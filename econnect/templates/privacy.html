What data we do NOT recopile

The list of data we do NOT recopile contains, but is not limited to:

  • Phone numbers
  • User current location
  • Names, ID numbers
  • Ages
  • User contacts

Data we DO recopile

  • Anonymized* chat history

* the chat history is anonymized randomly changing names, places and locations mentioned on it. Copies of the same history on diferent languages are also made.
